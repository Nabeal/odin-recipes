Vanilla Ice Cream

Description:

Topping the Chocolate Lava Cake with Vanilla Ice Cream sound heavenly!

Vanilla ice creams come in more varieties than you might think! It's made with five simple ingredients and turns into such a rich, creamy, delicious and decadent ice cream.

To make this ice cream, you will need these ingredients:

  • Egg Yolks
  • Sugar
  • Whole Milk
  • Heavy Cream
  • Vanilla Bean Paste or Vanilla Extract

    • Steps:

    1. Beat the sugar and eggs yolks
    2. Heat cream and milk
    3. Stream in the warm cream
    4. Heat the egg and cream mixture
    5. Churn
    6. Freeze
