Lava Chocolate Cake

Description:

Chocolate Lava Cake is a small, individual-sized cake. The center is slightly undercooked, on purpose, resulting in a gooey, melty, and decadent treat that oozes out when you cut into it.

This delicious treat will satisfy your savoury needs!

Only takes an 30 minutes and all you need is:

  • Butter
  • Bittersweet Chocolate
  • Eggs
  • Egg Yolks
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Flour

    • Steps:

    1. Preheat the oven
    2. Melt the chocolate and butter
    3. Combine the wet ingredients
    4. Add the flour
    5. Bake
    6. Serve
